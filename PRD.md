The Green Eyes App 

Making Big Buildings Smarter, Greener, and Cheaper to Run 

The Big Idea (Executive Summary) 
Imagine if your parents fined you $268 every single time you left the lights on in your bedroom. That's exactly what New York City is starting to do to big skyscrapers. If a building pollutes too much or wastes electricity, the city charges them massive fines. 
Green Eyes is an app that acts like a fitness tracker, but for a whole building. It helps building owners see exactly where they are wasting energy so they can fix it, save money, and help stop climate change. 

1.0 What's the Problem? 
NYC wants to be 100% clean energy by 2040. To force buildings to get greener, they passed strict new rules. Buildings that use dirty energy or waste electricity get hit with huge penalty fees. 
The problem? Most building owners are flying blind. They just get a massive energy bill at the end of the month and have no idea if the heater was left on with the windows open on the 10th floor. 
Who uses this app? 
● Building Owners: To avoid getting fined and save money. 
● Property Managers: The people who run the building day-to-day and need to know if stuff is broken. 

2.0 The Cool Features (How it Works) 
This is the core of Green Eyes. Here is exactly what the app does, explained simply The Building's "Reduction” Monitor (Primary Build)
● What it is: Modern buildings have a "brain" that controls air conditioning, heating, and big lights. Green Eyes plugs into this brain. 
● Why it's cool: Instead of guessing, you can look at the app and see if the AC is blasting in an empty room, or if a heater is working way too hard because it's broken. It's like checking the battery health on your phone.

2.1 The Solar Power Tracker 
● What it is: If a building puts solar panels on the roof, the app tracks exactly how much free electricity the sun is giving them.
● Why it's cool: It gamifies saving energy. The app shows you exactly how much money the solar panels saved you today, and how much pollution you stopped from going into the air. 

2.2 Smart Lights & Leak Detectors 
● What it is: Connects to motion-sensor lights and smart water pipes. ● Why it's cool: Have you ever walked into an empty hallway and the lights magically turn on? This app tracks those. It also spots water leaks instantly. If a pipe bursts at 2 AM, the app sends an alert before the building floods. 

2.3 The "Low Battery" Warning System 
● What it is: Push notifications for energy use. 
● Why it's cool: Just like your phone warns you when you hit 20% battery, Green Eyes sends a text message to the building manager if the building is using too much power and is about to get a massive fine from the city. They can fix the issue before the penalty hits. 

3.0 The Boring (But Important) Tech Stuff ● Where does it live? It's an app you can use on a computer, tablet, or smartphone.
● Is it safe? Yes. It uses the same high-level security and passwords as a banking app so hackers can't mess with a building's lights. 
● How fast is it? It updates every 15 minutes, so the info is always fresh. 

4.0 What Does Success Look Like? 
We'll know Green Eyes is a hit if: 
● People actually use it: We want 500 buildings using the app in the first year. 
● It saves money: The goal is to help a building cut its energy bill by 15%. 
● It stops fines: We want to save each building around $50,000 a year in city penalties. 
● It's fast: When something goes wrong (like a massive power spike), the app sends an alert to a phone in under 5 minutes.
